Vancouver, Canada Activities

Granville Island Public Market

If you are first timer in Vancouver, I highly recommend going to the Granville Island Public Market! My family and I went to this market and got these famous donuts from Lee's Donuts! It was so good we came back the next morning to get another box!

Stanley Park

Another fun idea is to tour the Stanley Park! It is this huge park with great viewpoints and picture opportunities. We got to look at the some of the Indigenous sites and see some horses!
