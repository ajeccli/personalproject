Favorite Desinations!

Honolulu, Hawaii

Visited Hawaii a handful of times, but my favorite time was going in the summer of 2023 when visiting a friend of mine.

Being able to visit Hawaii with a local and someone my age was a very different and immersive experience compared to my other times visiting.

I recommend going to the North Shore and planning a beach day there where it's less busy.

Seoul, South Korea

My favorite place to go! My mom's home and where most of my family lives. It's a beautiful place with rich culture and lots of new things to try.

I like spending time with my family and making and eating their homecooked meals. It just hits different when it comes from the origin and from family.

I recommend going to Gangnam and walking through during the day and night and doing karaoke and eating their street food.

Manhatten, New York

A frequent visit as some of my family lives in New York and my mom and dad lived there for a bit.

There truly is so much to do and see and the diverse group of people.

I recommend going through Central Park and getting a pizza at one of the street food vendors.
