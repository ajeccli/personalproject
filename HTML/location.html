Seoul, South Korea

This photo is from the Fortress Wall of Seoul, an old food market with vendors making traditional Korean dishes and desserts!

Other then spending time with my family, the food and culture was by far my favorite part of the trip! Authentic, affordable, and delicious!

Manhattan, New York

This photo is from brunch with my friends in SOHO!

Spending time to see New York through a local born and raised New Yorker was a different experience. And I got to spend that time with my close friends!!

Honolulu, Hawaii

This photo is from a local food truck in Kahuku-Keana, making the best shrimp plate!

Laying on the beach, going into the ocean, and the poke bowls from FoodsLands were my favorite memories!

San Francisco, California

This photo is from Union Square after sunset!

San Francisco is just an hour away from home for me, but it's not somewhere I go very often! The food and experience with my family was the best part of this quick visit!
